Services
by Hawa & Hussein LLP

Corporate Law

We conduct rigorous due diligence on all matters real estate and facilitate applications on mortgages, charges, leases, sales and transfers. We also handle wills and estate administration.

Banking, both Conventional and Islamic

We assist our clients to understand the regime in both systems and the risk involved therein, and, facilitate their matters on negotiable instruments, and other bearer instruments, joint ventures, mortgages and charges, hire purchase and debt collection.

Policy making and legislative drafting

We draft Bills and policy papers on behalf of various institutions including county governments, and conduct trainings to capacity build relevant personnel and legislators on the laws, policies, rules and procedures that govern their work.

Conveyancing

We conduct rigorous due diligence on all matters real estate and facilitate applications on mortgages, charges, leases, sales and transfers. We also handle wills and estate administration.

Family law

We advise our clients on divorce, judicial separation, maintenance cases and probate and administration causes.

Energy and Infrastructure

We advise diverse institutions and organizations, including community groups, on the feasibility of power generation projects, both conventional and renewable, project finance and, draft and review power purchase agreements and a myriad of agreements on mining and diverse infrastructure projects in East Africa.

Employment Law

We advise and facilitate on all matters employment such as work permits, employment contracts, occupational health and safety, pension, recruitment and retention of teams, and termination.

Medical law

We provide legal services to hospitals, health insurance companies, nursing homes, physician practices, medical device manufacturers, and others.

Research & Consultancy Services

We provide research services on behalf of institutions, both public and private, local and international, targeting marginalized communities primarily those in Northern Kenya, on issues surrounding the justice system, as well as the natural resource sector in Kenya.
