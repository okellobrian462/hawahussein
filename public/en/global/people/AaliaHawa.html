Aalia A.K. Hawa

  • Memberships and Awards Memberships and Awards

Aalia has expertise in commercial transactions, litigation as well as conveyancing having worked in leading firms, financial institutions and property agents in Kenya including Dunhill Consulting Limited, First Community Bank Ltd, Imran Dhanji & Company Adovates, Omulele and Tollo Advocates and Chaudhri and Associates Advocates. She has garnered experience in areas pertaining to debt recoveries, judicial reviews, due diligence of real estate as well as other commercial transactions, employment law and handling claims on the same before appropriate courts and tribunals in Kenya.

She has also excelled in providing sound legal advice and strategy to various corporates and entities to secure their interests. She further has expertise in conducting audits on new and upcoming Kenyan legislation and their impacts to enable Clients explore the best legal strategies in furthering their objectives..
